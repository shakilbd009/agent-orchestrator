# BRD-21: Notifications and Alerts

**Project:** agent-orchestrator  
**Domain:** UX  
**Phase:** 2  
**Owner:** developer  
**Status:** placeholder  

---

## Goal

Notify users of important events that require their attention, including approval requests, gate completions, blockers, and deployment status changes.

---

## Problem Statement

Users cannot monitor the platform continuously. They need timely notifications so they can participate at decision points without constantly polling the system.

---

## Scope

### In Scope
- Notification events and triggers
- Notification delivery channels (in-app, email)
- Notification preferences per user
- Read/unread state and dismissal

### Out of Scope
- Push notifications to mobile devices
- SMS or third-party messaging integrations

- BRD-03 (Dashboard)
- Feature flag: `notifications`
