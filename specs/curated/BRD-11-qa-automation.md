# BRD-11: QA Automation

**Project:** agent-orchestrator  
**Domain:** Quality  
**Phase:** 1  
**Owner:** qa

---

## Goal

Define how automated QA tests are generated, maintained, and executed as part of the quality gate pipeline.

---

## Problem Statement

Manual QA is slow, inconsistent, and does not scale. The platform must generate and maintain test coverage automatically.

---

## Scope

### In Scope
- Test generation from acceptance criteria
- Test execution in CI pipeline
- Failure reporting and triage
- Test coverage thresholds

### Out of Scope
- Manual test case management
- Exploratory testing workflows
