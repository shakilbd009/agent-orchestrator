## Goal

Define the structured process by which business requirements are captured, refined, reviewed, and promoted to approved status.

---

## Problem Statement

Without a defined BRD authoring process, requirements drift between sessions, decisions are not recorded, and scope control breaks down.

---

## Scope

### In Scope
- BRD template and required sections
- Review and approval workflow
- Version tracking and change history
- Draft → reviewed → approved lifecycle

### Out of Scope
- Automated BRD generation from user prompts
- BRD storage or search infrastructure
