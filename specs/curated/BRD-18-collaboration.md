# BRD-18: Collaboration and Team Management

**Project:** agent-orchestrator  
**Domain:** Workflow  
**Phase:** 2  
**Owner:** pm  
**Status:** placeholder  

---

## Goal

Enable multiple human users to collaborate on the same project, with role-based permissions, shared workspaces, and comment threads.

---

## Problem Statement

Solo-user projects do not reflect real agency or consultancy workflows where multiple stakeholders review, approve, and contribute.

---

## Scope

### In Scope
- User roles (owner, editor, reviewer, viewer)
- Project invitation and access management
- Comment threads on requirements and decisions
- Activity feed for team actions

### Out of Scope
- Real-time collaborative editing
- External SSO integration

---

## Dependencies

- BRD-17 (Multi-Tenant Isolation)
- Feature flag: `collaboration`
