# BRD-16: Risk Tracking

**Project:** agent-orchestrator  
**Domain:** Governance  
**Phase:** 1  
**Owner:** pm  
**Status:** placeholder  

---

## Goal

Define how risks are identified, categorized, scored, owner-assigned, and tracked through mitigation or acceptance.

---

## Problem Statement

Untracked risks accumulate silently until they cause project failures. The platform must surface risks proactively with enough context for human decision-making.

---

## Scope

### In Scope
- Risk identification during planning and execution
- Severity and likelihood scoring
- Owner assignment and mitigation plan
- Risk status tracking (open, mitigated, accepted, closed)

### Out of Scope
- Quantitative risk modeling or Monte Carlo simulation
- Automated risk identification from code or requirements
