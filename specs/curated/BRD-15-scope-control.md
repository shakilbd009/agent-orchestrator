# BRD-15: Scope Control

**Project:** agent-orchestrator  
**Domain:** Governance  
**Phase:** 1  
**Owner:** pm  
**Status:** placeholder  

---

## Goal

Define how the platform distinguishes between approved scope, pending changes, and out-of-scope requests, and how scope changes are evaluated for impact before adoption.

---

## Problem Statement

Mid-project scope expansion is a primary cause of delivery delays and budget overruns. The platform must give users visibility into the impact of change requests before accepting them.

---

## Scope

### In Scope
- Scope baseline definition (approved scope at project start)
- Change request format and impact assessment
- Scope comparison view (baseline vs current vs proposed)
- Out-of-scope flagging

### Out of Scope
- Automated scope change approval
- Budget or timeline estimation
