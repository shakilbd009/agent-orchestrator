# BRD-19: Decision and Artifact History

**Project:** agent-orchestrator  
**Domain:** Workflow  
**Phase:** 2  
**Owner:** architect  
**Status:** placeholder  

---

## Goal

Maintain a complete versioned history of all decisions, BRD changes, and artifact modifications so teams can audit how the project evolved.

---

## Problem Statement

Without version history, teams cannot understand why a decision was made, compare before and after states, or recover from unintended changes.

---

## Scope

### In Scope
- Version tracking for BRDs
- Decision log with change attribution
- Artifact diff and restore capability
- Audit log export

### Out of Scope
- Automated decision recommendation based on history
- Long-term archival or compliance retention policies

## Dependencies

- BRD-07 (BRD Authoring Workflow)
- Feature flag: `change-history`
